Stay in the heart of Sarajevo
Situated within only 50 m from the Gazi Husrev Bey's Mosque and the Old Orthodox Church in Sarajevo's historical part Baščaršija, Hotel Garni Konak offers free Wi-Fi access
All rooms are air conditioned and come with flat-screen satellite TV. The private bathrooms are equipped with hairdryers and toiletries. Guests can start their day with a breakfast buffet served every morning in the cosy breakfast room. Garni Hotel Konak is situated within the pedestrian area with numerous dining and shopping opportunities. Guests can book trips and tours around Sarajevo and other towns in Bosnia and Herzegovina, as well as to Croatia and Montenegro. The Garni Konak offers room service, luggage storage and safety boxes. Shuttle service from the airport to the hotel can be arranged upon previous request. Charges may apply. This is our guests' favourite part of Sarajevo, according to independent reviews. This property also has one of the best-rated locations in Sarajevo! Guests are happier about it compared to other properties in the area.
Couples particularly like the location — they rated it 9.7 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
